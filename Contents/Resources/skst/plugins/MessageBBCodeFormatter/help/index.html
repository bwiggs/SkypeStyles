To use BBCode markup language in your messages, simply surround some text with the BBCode markups as in any forum

Example:

This is [b]bold[/b], this is [color=red]red[/color], [i]italic[/i], [u]underlined[/u].
You can combine tags (first opened, first closed) [b]this is bold and [i]this is bold and italic[/i][/b]
You can also play with the alignement of the text: [right]This text will be aligned on the right[/right]
